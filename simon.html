HOW TO PLAY:
Simon Says is an game of memory and skill!
A random sequence of colours will flash on your screen,
remember these colours and the order they present themselves in...
Once the colours stop flashing, you will have to click each colour as they appeared... In order, with a time limit.
Up for the challenge? Press Play!
